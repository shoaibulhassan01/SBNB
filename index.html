S BNB

S BNB, short for Synthesis BNB Chain, is a groundbreaking and innovative blockchain platform built to expand the capabilities of BNB. Merging the strengths of existing BNB functionalities with new-age solutions, S BNB promises to be the bridge between current DeFi systems and futuristic blockchain technologies.

Roadmap

Concept & Whitepaper*

- Formation of the core team. - Development of the whitepaper detailing S BNB's vision and mission.

Initial Development & Testnet Launch*

- Kickstart of the Synthesis BNB Chain development. - Launch of the testnet for preliminary trials.

Mainnet Launch & Wallet Integration*

- Mainnet goes live. - Integration with major cryptocurrency wallets.

Partnerships & DApps Development*

- Establishing strategic partnerships with key players in the crypto industry. - Promoting and supporting the development of decentralized applications on the S BNB platform.

Exchange Listings & Marketing Push*

- S BNB token gets listed on major exchanges. - Intensive marketing campaigns to boost awareness and adoption.

Scaling & Optimization*

- Implementation of layer-2 solutions for scalability. - Regular updates and optimizations based on user feedback.

Token Distribution:

  • Public Sale: 35%
